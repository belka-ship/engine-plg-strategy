01
Proactive Orchestration

Before Alex opens a browser.

02
Traveler DNA Match

Engine selects a hotel which is part of the chain Alex loves. It's exactly a 10-minute walk from both client offices and features the premium gym he requires.

08 — Cross-Functional Launch Plan

Launching the Recommendation Engine.

Product Marketing

Positioning AI-powered booking to SMB buyers.

Create launch messaging and sales enablement materials that translate recommendation intelligence and agentic booking into buyer language. Build demo scripts, competitive battle cards, and pilot case studies.

Sales / Account Mgmt

Selecting pilot accounts and training AEs on the new demo flow.

Identify high-volume enterprise accounts for phased rollout. Train account teams on demoing personalized recommendations, the "Ask about this hotel" panel, and Slack agent workflows.

Customer Support

Building the AI support playbook from day one.

Train agents on how the recommendation engine ranks results and how agentic actions execute. Create escalation paths for edge cases — wrong suggestions, failed bookings, Slack integration issues. Develop traveler-facing FAQ: "How does Engine choose my hotels?"

Legal & Compliance

Clearing AI disclosure and data-access gates.

Review AI/ML disclosure requirements for enterprise contracts. Approve data-access scopes for Slack, Gmail, and Calendar integrations. Draft an AI transparency addendum covering preference collection, recommendation logic, and traveler data handling under GDPR.

Supplier Ops

Ensuring inventory depth and content quality.

Guarantee the recommendation engine has real-time availability and competitive rates across priority markets. Audit hotel content quality — images, descriptions, amenity tags — that directly feed the ranking algorithm and "why recommended" display.

Data & Analytics

Standing up the evaluation and experimentation pipeline.

Build the LLM-as-a-judge + human audit pipeline to monitor recommendation quality against the 95% pass-rate target. Instrument A/B testing for ranking algorithms. Track conversion, click-through, and booking-completion metrics per recommendation surface.
